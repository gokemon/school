About Life Lessons

Life Lessons is an ASL community created to teach American Sign Language by showing video lessons and providing a chat tool for student teacher chats. This site is a free, and you can use it for any personal or learning purpose.

Life Lessons includes full Sign Language along with basics, and comprehension for easy learning.
